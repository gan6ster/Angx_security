ABOUT US

ANGGH SECURITY" LLC, which provides security and bodyguard service, was founded on January 15, 2021, the organization provides the service of bodyguards, licensed by the police, qualified by the organization. We provide security service under a contract with individuals and legal entities.

We provide the most popular security services

  • » Stationary service at the checkpoint.

  • » Itinerant service in the protected area.

  • » Temporary service with object and subject.

  • » Control of long and short areas in Armenia.

  • » Maintaining visa regime in the protected area.

  • » Monitoring of video surveillance devices.

  • » Installation of surveillance devices․

  • » Installation of signaling devices and control by rapid response team.

  • » Protection of objects and property․

  • » Ensuring the security of the tenants in the objects․

  • » Providing Mass Events․

  • » Professional Consulting for organizing security․
